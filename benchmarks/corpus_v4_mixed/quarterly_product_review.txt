Quarterly Product Review - Q2

Highlights:
- Search latency dropped from 1.8 seconds to 0.9 seconds at p95.
- File indexing throughput improved by 32 percent after batch chunk writes.
- The WhatsApp pilot expanded from 500 to 1,400 active users.

Risks:
- Semantic search relevance remains unvalidated on multilingual documents.
- Reranker token cost increased after switching from snippets to larger chunks.

Planned work:
- Build an offline relevance benchmark before changing retrieval weights again.
- Limit reranking to cases where lexical confidence is weak.
- Add per-stage timing for lexical retrieval, semantic retrieval, fusion, and reranking.
