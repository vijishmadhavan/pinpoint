Customer Success Playbook

Renewal risk signals:
- Weekly usage drops for three consecutive weeks.
- Executive sponsor leaves the account.
- Critical support escalations remain unresolved for more than seven days.

Quarterly business review template:
- Outcomes delivered
- Adoption and engagement metrics
- Renewal timeline and procurement owners

Escalation path:
- Customer success manager owns first response.
- Solutions architect joins for integration blockers.
- Finance approves discounts above 12 percent.
