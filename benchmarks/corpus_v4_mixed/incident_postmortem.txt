Incident Postmortem: Messaging Delay on April 12

Summary:
The notification pipeline experienced a backlog after a queue consumer stalled during a schema migration.

Impact:
- 18 percent of outbound WhatsApp messages were delayed.
- Median delivery delay peaked at 27 minutes.

Corrective actions:
1. Add exponential backoff for worker retries.
2. Introduce a canary migration check before rollout.
3. Publish a shift handover checklist for the on-call engineer.
4. Page the platform team when queue depth exceeds 50,000 jobs.
