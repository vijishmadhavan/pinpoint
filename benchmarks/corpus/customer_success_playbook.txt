Customer Success Playbook

Renewal risk signals:
- Usage drops for three consecutive weeks.
- Executive sponsor leaves the customer account.
- Support escalations remain unresolved for more than seven days.

Quarterly business review template:
- Start with outcomes delivered.
- Review adoption metrics.
- Confirm renewal timeline and procurement owners.

Escalation path:
- Customer success manager owns the first response.
- Solutions architect joins for integration blockers.
- Finance approves discount requests over 12 percent.

Expansion strategy:
- Offer analytics add-on after adoption exceeds 70 percent.
- Propose multilingual support for accounts serving India and the Middle East.
