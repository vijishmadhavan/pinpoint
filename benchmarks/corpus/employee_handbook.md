# Employee Handbook

Remote work is allowed three days per week with manager approval.

Vacation policy:
- Full-time employees receive 24 days of annual leave.
- Carry-forward is limited to 5 unused days.
- Leave requests should be submitted at least two weeks in advance.

Parental leave:
- Primary caregiver leave is 16 weeks.
- Secondary caregiver leave is 4 weeks.

Security basics:
- Never share MFA codes.
- Report suspicious logins to the security desk within 30 minutes.

Onboarding checklist:
- Laptop issued by IT
- Password manager access
- Payroll and tax forms completed
